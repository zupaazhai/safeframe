Billboard Rising Star Ad

The Billboard is a large billboard running the full width of the page with full close-ability.

In this example the Billboard ad pushes down page content to place itself front and center. The user can close the ad by clicking the "Close" button to dismiss the ad. A small "Show ad" link is left in case the user wishes to re-display the ad.
